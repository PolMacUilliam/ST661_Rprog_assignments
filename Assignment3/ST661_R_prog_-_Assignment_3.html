(a) Pose a question relating to the data.

How soon into the Heptathlon (7 events) is it possible to see a winning lead. Is it possible to discern any patterns in the scoring which might indicate which athletes are stronger in which events.

(b) Using ggplot, construct a visualisation which helps to answer this question.

(c) Answer your question, based on the visualisation.

Based on the plots of the top 10 and bottom 10 athletes (in terms of overall points) scored, it can be seen that an early lead for the eventual medalists is discernible very early on after the 3rd event. The eventual Gold medalist lead from the 3rd event until the end - in fact coming out on top in 4 of the 7 events.

(d) Explain why you selected this particular visualisation.

We chose this particular visualisation for a number of reasons. Firstly, it showed an interesting perspective on the overall accumulation of points as each individual event concluded. It is also possible to see the proportional effect of the points scored per event as they accumulated. Finally the form we chose to a certain extent also mimics the progress of the Heptathlon. This makes it easy to interpret for any audience.
